Food is my passion

Hi and thanks for visiting my restaurant website! My name is Isak and I'm from the island Gotland, in Sweden. I moved to Nice in 2009 to learn french and discover french cuisine. Soon I got employment at La Rossettisserie where I became the chef.

After one fantastic year in Nice I went travelling and worked through restaurants in Saint Petersburg, Paris and Stockholm. In 2011 I became a member of the Swedish Junior Culinary Team. We competed in the Culinary Olympics 2012 became Olympic Gold champions! 

I've always dreamed about running my own restaurant and serve my own food. This dream came true last year when I opened ISI, a private hidden restaurant in the heart of the old town in Nice.

Along with the restaurant I do private events, catering, recipe writing and consulting. Contact me at info@isirestaurant.com for any questions!

Have a delicious day!

/Isak Oldenburg
