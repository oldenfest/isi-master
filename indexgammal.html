Welcome to ISI, a hidden restaurant in the heart of the old town in Nice. It holds only 12 covers at one big table next to the kitchen.

"I believe that great food starts with great products"

All our produce comes from the local producers and differs from day to day. Thats why we cook with a spontaneous and open mind to truly be able to follow the season and serve a unique menu every day.
